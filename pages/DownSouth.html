Down South

Overview

The southern coastline of Sri Lanka is home to some of the most beautiful and diverse beaches in the country. These beaches along the southern coast of Sri Lanka cater to a variety of interests, from water sports and nightlife to quiet relaxation and nature exploration. The region is a true paradise for beach lovers, offering some of the best coastal experiences in the country.

Attractions areas in Down South

Hikkaduwa Beach

Hikkaduwa is renowned for its coral reefs, which are some of the most accessible in Sri Lanka. The Hikkaduwa Coral Sanctuary, part of the larger Hikkaduwa National Park, is a protected area where you can snorkel and see a variety of colorful corals, tropical fish, and other marine life.major surfing destination, with waves suitable for both beginners and experienced surfersThe beach is lined with bars, restaurants, and cafes, offering a lively nightlife scene

Ahangama Beach

Ahangama Beach is perfect for travelers who prefer a quieter, more authentic beach experience, with the added benefit of great surfing and the opportunity to observe traditional Sri Lankan culture up close. Whether you're looking to surf, relax, or explore local traditions, Ahangama offers a unique and serene escape on Sri Lanka's southern coast.

Weligama Beach

Weligama Beach is one of the most charming and picturesque beaches on Sri Lanka's southern coast. Weligama Beach is particularly famous for its beginner-friendly surf conditions. The gentle, consistent waves make it an ideal spot for those new to surfing. Several surf schools are dotted along the beach,Just off the coast of Weligama Beach lies Taprobane Island, a small, private island with a single, luxurious villa. While surfing is the main attraction, the waters around Weligama also offer opportunities for snorkeling and diving.
