Matale

Overview

Matale, located in Sri Lanka's Central Province, is a region rich in natural beauty, cultural heritage, and historical significance. Known for attractions like the Aluvihare Rock Temple, the picturesque Sembuwatta Lake, and the biodiversity-rich Knuckles Mountain Range, Matale offers a unique blend of experiences. Visitors can explore ancient temples, hike scenic trails, and enjoy the tranquility of spice gardens and waterfalls. Meemure village

Mimure Village, located near Sri Lanka's Knuckles Mountain Range, is a hidden gem perfect for tourists seeking tranquility and natural beauty. With its lush forests, picturesque landscapes, and cascading waterfalls, Mimure offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Visitors can enjoy scenic hikes, explore the rich biodiversity of the surrounding area, and experience the charm of traditional rural life. Ideal for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers, Mimure provides an authentic and peaceful retreat in one of Sri Lanka's most scenic regions.
